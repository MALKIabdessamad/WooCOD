== Description ==

**WooCOD – Cash on Delivery for WooCommerce** is a lightweight plugin designed to help WooCommerce store owners offer **Cash on Delivery (COD)** as a payment option directly on the product page.

With WooCOD, customers can select the **“Buy with Cash on Delivery”** button without having to go through the cart and checkout process, simplifying their shopping experience and boosting conversions. This plugin is specifically **tested and optimized for the Astra theme**, providing seamless integration and styling. For other themes, compatibility may vary.

**Features:**
- Adds a **“Buy with Cash on Delivery”** button to product pages.
- Skip cart and checkout, allowing customers to order in one click.
- Customizable button text and styling.
- Easy to install and configure.
- **Tested and compatible** with the Astra theme.
  
**Note:** While WooCOD works perfectly with the Astra theme, **other themes may require additional customization**. Please test the plugin on your theme before purchasing to ensure full compatibility.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `woocod` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to **WooCommerce > Settings > WooCOD** to customize the button text, colors, and other settings.
4. Enjoy a one-click COD checkout experience on your product pages!
